Your privacy and security are our top priorities. Learn how we protect your personal information and documents.

Last Updated: August 26, 2025

1. Introduction

OSS is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our government document application services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information necessary for government document processing:

  • Full name, date of birth, and contact information
  • Government-issued identification numbers (SSN, passport numbers, etc.)
  • Address and residential history
  • Employment and educational information
  • Emergency contact details

2.2 Documents and Files

Users upload various documents required for government applications, including:

  • Identity verification documents (driver's license, passport, etc.)
  • Proof of residence and citizenship documents
  • Supporting certificates and legal documents
  • Photographs for official identification purposes
  • Financial documents when required for specific applications

2.3 Payment Information

For processing fees and service charges, we collect:

  • Credit card or debit card information
  • Billing address and contact information
  • Bank account details for direct transfers (when applicable)
  • Payment history and transaction records

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Document Processing: To complete and submit government document applications on your behalf
  • Identity Verification: To verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Payment Processing: To process service fees and government filing fees
  • Customer Support: To provide assistance and updates on your applications
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with government regulations and legal requirements
  • Service Improvement: To enhance our platform and services (using anonymized data only)

4. Payment Security

4.1 Payment Processing

We partner with industry-leading payment processors who maintain the highest security standards:

  • All payment data is encrypted using TLS 1.3 protocol
  • We are PCI DSS Level 1 compliant
  • Credit card information is tokenized and never stored on our servers
  • Multi-factor authentication for payment processing

4.2 Financial Data Protection

Your financial information receives additional protection:

  • Separate encrypted databases for payment information
  • Regular security audits by third-party security firms
  • Fraud detection and prevention systems
  • Immediate notification of suspicious activities

üí≥ Payment Security Guarantee

We guarantee that your payment information is processed through secure, encrypted channels and is never stored in plain text on our systems.

5. Document Security

Your uploaded documents are protected through multiple security layers:

  • End-to-End Encryption: Documents are encrypted from upload to deletion
  • Secure Storage: Files are stored in SOC 2 Type II certified data centers
  • Access Controls: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
  • Audit Trails: Complete logs of who accessed what documents and when
  • Automatic Deletion: Documents are permanently deleted after application completion

6. Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Government Agencies: Required information is shared with relevant government offices for document processing
  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist in payment processing and document delivery
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize information sharing

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for the following periods:

  • Application Data: Retained for 7 years as required by government regulations
  • Uploaded Documents: Automatically deleted 90 days after application completion
  • Payment Records: Kept for 7 years for tax and audit purposes
  • Account Information: Retained until you request account deletion

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

9. Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures including:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Multi-factor authentication for all user accounts
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Employee background checks and security training
  • 24/7 security monitoring and incident response
  • Regular data backups with encrypted storage

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through our platform. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
